Who we are ?

"Great things are done by a series of small things brought together"

Coding society is a community of students highly enthusiastic about coding,web designing and all other aspects of programming. Programming is not limited to only computer science and related fields. Every competent engineer needs to know how to program. Students work all year long in building projects, organising workshops and thriving to create a change. Our aim is to propagate the enthusiasm for coding in the institute and especially amongnst freshman. We believe that every student here at ABVGIET should have opportuniy in giving a platform to the young engineers to convert their theoretical knowledge into useful innovative projects and develop passion for it.

What We Do ?

Web Development

Web application development is the process and practice of developing web applications.

App Development

App Development refers to the creation of computer applications for use on mobile devices

Open Source Track

Open source software is software in which the source code used to create the program is freely available for the public to view, edit, and redistribute (e.g., Linux)

Internet Of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) describes physical objects that are embedded with sensors, processing ability, software,
that connect and exchange data with other devices over the Internet.

AR Development

Augmented reality refers to any technology that 'augments'
the user's perception of their environment.

DevOps & Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is a technology that uses the internet for storing
& managing data on remote servers, and then access data via the internet.
